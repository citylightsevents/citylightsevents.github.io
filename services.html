LIVE PERFORMANCE

 

At Citylights, we work continuously to expand our group of talented artists. If you own a live venue, we'd like to bring these artists to your audience. We are confident that we can provide quality programming for any venue size, location, or genre requirement.

 

 

EVENT CINEMA

 

For venues which are equipped for projection, we distribute a range of recorded and broadcast content. We also assist with marketing our programming to help you maximize your attendance. Contact us to find out how you can supplement your cinema box office with our range of art based programming.

PRODUCTION

 

Whether you are a new artist seeking exposure for the first time or an established industry veteran, Citylights can help you push the boundaries of your act. Our multimedia production team can help you publish your content in multiple formats. In addition to traditional media, our talent for merging music, cinematography, and the visual arts will prepare your work for distribution in a diverse set of venues.

 

 

DISTRIBUTION

 

Citylights' core mission is to help artists seek out novel audiences. We value the importance of live performance in a digitally dominant world. We feel strongly that the combination of live music and large format visuals presents a compelling experience for modern audiences. To this end, we have entered into partnerships with a range of cinema and stage venues. We are confident that our skilled production, distribution, and promotion chain can bring success to your talent. For those artists who hold ready produced, recorded content, contact us to find out how we can place your work in front of more people than ever.
