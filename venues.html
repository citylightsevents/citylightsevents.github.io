CITYLIGHTS FOCUSES ON PROJECTION EQUIPPED LIVE PERFORMANCE VENUES, PREDOMINANTLY DUAL PURPOSE EVENT CINEMAS.

 

Citylights' mission is to market the combination of live music and large format visuals as a modern entertainment medium. We see converted dual use cinemas as the future of popular musical entertainment, one which bridges the gap between dedicated concert venues and disseminated personal consumption.

 

Citylights is based in Shanghai, China. We have partnered with cinemas across the country to build a new generation of live performance capable venues. If you own a venue and are interested in bringing fresh creativity to your business, get in touch with us to discuss a retrofit.
